Notifies Topcoder new challenges dailly through mail

  • chevron_right You can add tags(Ex: Java, NodeJs) to filter out challenges based on those tags.
  • chevron_right You can change time to get notified at that time(Default: none).
  • chevron_right Challenges once notifed to you won't get notified again next day unless there are new challenges.

Please Note:

  • chevron_right Notification Schdeule Time updated to time less than 30 min from now will be notified from tomorrow.
  • chevron_right Actual notification time may vary by +/- 10 min, due to the tunning of app scheduler to reduce server load.
  • chevron_right Challenges are filtered out by regex matching tags in description, be sure to give all variation of tag like (NodeJs, Node.Js)
  • chevron_right To un-schedule click /un-schedule link in dashboard.
